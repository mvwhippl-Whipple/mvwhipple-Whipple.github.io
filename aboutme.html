Jimmy Whipple - Metaphysical Witchdoctor

Jewelry is a from of self-expression for me that I find as a way to channel my spirituality. I began creating pieces inspired by Native American artwork--decorated skulls, smudge fans, and rapé pipes. Through experimentation I was led to experience working with numerous techniques, materials, and people all of which inspired me. With the skills I acquired, I began to understand the bountiful creativity offered by lapidary and silversmithing. Each piece leading me to knew inspiration and an understanding of myself

For me, jewelry should be worn with intention and crafted with purpose. Every custom piece is inspired by the spirit of the wearer and forged in a process that honors that.

The Materials

The majority of materials are locally sourced--found personally in the wilderness, sourced from local vendors, etc. For those that are not, they come from small sustainable businesses. When it comes to animal products, I believe that every piece of an animal should be used to honor its life, so I often incorporate usual animal-derived materials. Metals are of the highest accessory grade and all gemstones are polished, shaped, and examined for quality.

The Process

There are many pieces that are personal projects available through my shop. All pieces are completely handmade and unique, thus there will never be one like it again. If you are interested in custom pieces, leave a message in the query section of this website. Whether you have no idea or an exact concept in your head, I will work with you through the design process to give you the exact product you want. Work times vary greatly depending on the details of the piece, but can be discussed personally. Shipping times are a standard 2-3 day through USPS priority shipping. International shipping is best discussed with me personally.

The Work

My work is diverse. There are few things I limit myself to creating. I have done ceremonial bone chestplates, gothic skull candelabras, ornamented and elaborate necklaces, I've incorporated ashes of loved once into some pieces, I've used the remains of beloved past pets, and I've even done used blessed ceremonial tools to ward homes of negative spirits.
