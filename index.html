Metal remembers what hands forget.

Handcrafted jewelry.

There is something in metal that resists forgetting. Lost Dragonfly Studios creates handcrafted jewelry at the intersection of the natural and the symbolic through pieces that carry weight in both material and meaning. Made one at a time, with intention and purpose.

Each piece begins as raw material and becomes something personal. Whether you're drawn to the organic, the ornate, or somewhere between, every item in the studio is shaped entirely by hand. No two exactly alike, no shortcuts taken.
